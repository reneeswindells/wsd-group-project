Activities for well-being

Cosy reset: Light a candle, dim your lights, put on some calm music you like, clean up your desk. It instantly changes the atmosphere.

Mini Pamper session: Put on a face mask, take a warm shower, get into comfy clothes, put on your favourite film!

Screen free time: Read something for pleasure, a magazine, a book. Make a snack, treat yourself to one of your fave foods!

Move your body: Look up a yoga video on Youtube, or a dance fit video to get yourself moving

Helplines and Support

If you ever feel like you need to talk to someone, there are helplines that are there for you.

📞Samaritans – 116 123A 24/7 confidential listening service for anyone who needs someone to talk to.

📞 Shout (Text Service) – Text SHOUT to 85258A free, confidential text line for moments when you need immediate support.

📞 Mind Infoline – 0300 123 3393Provides information and guidance on mental health problems and where to get help.
